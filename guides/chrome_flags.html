Chrome Flags are experimental features that can enhance your browsing experience.

  • Parallel Downloading: Boosts download speed. chrome://flags/#enable-parallel-downloading
  • Dark Mode: Activates dark mode for websites. chrome://flags/#enable-force-dark
  • Tab Groups Save: Saves grouped tabs for later. chrome://flags/#tab-groups-save
